Quran Short

Automated Quran short videos for social platforms.

Quran Short creates short-form Quran videos with recitation, verse text, translation, and curated visual backgrounds. The service is used to generate vertical videos and publish them to connected social media accounts.

Privacy Policy Terms of Service

What it does

The app prepares Quran clips with audio recitation, Arabic text, translation, hashtags, and social-ready formatting for short video platforms.

How it is used

The owner connects social accounts, reviews generated content when needed, and publishes educational Quran videos for viewers who prefer short-form media.
