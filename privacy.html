Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Quran Short is used to create and publish Quran short-form videos for social media platforms. This policy explains what information may be processed when the service is connected to third-party platforms.

Information we use

  • Connected platform authorization tokens needed to publish content.
  • Basic profile information returned by connected platform APIs.
  • Generated video metadata such as titles, descriptions, and publish logs.

How information is used

  • To authenticate the account owner with connected social platforms.
  • To upload and publish Quran short videos on approved accounts.
  • To keep simple history logs that help avoid duplicate content patterns.

Data sharing

Quran Short does not sell personal data. Information is only shared with the third-party services that are explicitly connected by the account owner for publishing and platform authorization.

Data retention

Authorization tokens and publishing metadata may be stored for as long as they are needed to operate the automation workflow, unless the owner removes access or deletes the related files or secrets.
